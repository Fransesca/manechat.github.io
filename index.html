Rules

Welcome to Manechat! Keep it family friendly and SFW. Breaking the rules will result in being muted, kicked, or banned. Fundamentally, our rules are mostly the same as the /r/mylittlepony subreddit rules, subject to moderator discretion.

  1. Be pony-like and kind to other members.
    1. Trolling, impersonation, and similar acts are not acceptable!
    2. Follow common courtesy in voice chat. Keep it SFW, do not disturb in-progress games with off-topic chatter, and properly calibrate your microphone.
    3. Users that consistently create negative experiences for other users will be removed, even if no singular event would be considered severe.
  2. No offensive, controversial, or NSFW avatars, pictures, emotes, or topics. This means no politics or religion.
  3. All discussion, pictures, and any related content about the upcoming My Little Pony: A New Generation movie must be kept in the appropriately named spoiler channel #g5-spoilers-leaks. If it was shown in the trailer you can also use #g5-trailer.
    1. MLP content that airs ahead of time is considered spoilers, even if it comes from an official source.
      Examples: G5 movie airs in Hong Kong before Netflix release, or a book comes out that spoils the plot of an unreleased movie/episode
    2. Do not carelessly spoil important plot points of any media in general, regardless of its age.
  4. Shitposting, spamming, and low-effort memes are prohibited. Don't use excessively illegible characters in usernames or text.
    1. Do not exercise excessive roleplay anywhere on the server. This is not the place for RP.
  5. Links to other Discord servers, patreons, gofundmes and similar are not allowed. This includes PMing members of the server with spam.
    1. Links to streams, channels, and other self-promotion should be posted only once per 24 hours.
    2. Advertising commissions, YCHs, and other art related services in exchange for money should be kept to #my-creations and no more often than once a week.
    3. Please consider contacting a Princess if someone on the server, without asking for it, PMs you with links of advertisement, publicity, self-promotion or similar.
  6. We are not a support group. If you need help, please seek out the help of a professional in your area.
    International List of Crisis Hotlines

Do not enforce the rules or try to regulate behaviour, that is exclusively the Princesses' responsibility. If you think or feel a rule is being breached, or you have concerns, PM a Princess without worry.

All Princess decisions are final.

  • Princesses have full discretion to remove content or users deemed unpleasant or unwelcomed by community members.
  • Princesses have full discretion to change channel topics.

Respect Discord's Terms of Service.

Abusing or exploiting bugs in Sweetie Bot or Discord itself is a bannable offense.

If you'd like to appeal a ban or otherwise communicate with the Princesses outside of Discord, you can fill this form and we'll get back to you.

Channels

Certain channels are used for certain activities and discussions. Temporary channels may be created for certain events. Do not clutter the general chat with content that belongs somewhere else.

#announcements The Princesses use this channel to communicate important information. If there's a new message make sure to read it.
#manechat The main channel for general purpose.
#tailchat The second channel for when #manechat is too busy for you.
#server-feedback For discussing Manechat, possible changes to it, and the server's direction for the future.
#bots-channel For playing with and abusing the bot.
#mlp-theory For discussion of lore, story, or theories involving MLP.
#g5-spoilers For spoilers and leaks of anything that comes after G4. User discretion is advised.
#spoilers-nonmlp Spoiler channel for media that isn't MLP.
#gaming For talking about video games, tabletop games, card games and the like.
#science-and-tech For conversations about hardware, software, math, science and such.
#my-creations To share, comment and discuss the creative project you are working on.
#fanfics-and-comics For fanfics and comics, also where the Reading Club has its discussions.
#sports-and-fitness For talking about sporting events, fitness & exercise, or related topics.
#events For conventions and other events.
#booru-site For anything related to our image booru site, Manebooru. Server rules apply, especially Rule 2.
#booru-support If you need help with anything Manebooru related from the staff.
#voice-text-chat Use this when responding to VC.
